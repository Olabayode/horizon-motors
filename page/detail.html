Our Services

Vehicle Sales

At Horizon Motors, our vehicle sales program is built on trust, transparency, and premium quality. We offer a curated selection of brand-new models along with certified pre-owned vehicles that undergo extensive multi-point inspections. Each vehicle is evaluated for performance, safety, and long-term reliability, ensuring you receive exceptional value. Our consultants provide personalized guidance, helping you compare features, financing options, and ownership benefits to find the perfect car for your lifestyle. Choosing Horizon means choosing excellence from the very first step of your automotive journey.

Vehicle Rentals

Our rental fleet provides flexible solutions for drivers who need short-term or long-term mobility. Whether you need a compact city car, a family-sized SUV, or a premium luxury model, we ensure every vehicle is meticulously maintained and fully sanitized for your comfort. Our rental plans include transparent pricing, unlimited mileage options, and seamless pick-up and drop-off experiences. From weekend trips to business travel, Horizon Motors delivers convenience, reliability, and the freedom to explore without limitations.

Wraps & Repairs

The Horizon Motors service center is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic systems and a skilled team of certified technicians. We offer everything from routine maintenance—such as oil changes and brake servicing—to complex mechanical repairs. For customers looking to personalize their vehicle, we provide premium vinyl wraps, detailing, body restoration, and cosmetic upgrades. Our technicians use high-grade materials and industry-leading techniques to protect your vehicle’s integrity while enhancing its appearance. Your car leaves our workshop performing better, looking newer, and feeling refined.
